Fried hilsa fish served over rice

Ingredients

Fried Hilsa fish served over rice, a popular Bengali dish, primarily requires hilsa fish, rice, and mustard oil. Other ingredients include salt, turmeric, green chilies, and sometimes red chilies for flavor and garnish.

Halva served with raisins and nuts

Ingredients

Halva, when served with raisins and nuts, typically includes a base of semolina or tahini, along with sugar, and additions like raisins, almonds, cashews, or pistachios. Some recipes also incorporate spices like cardamom, saffron, or cinnamon, and may use ghee or other fats.

A bowl of mutton biryani

Ingredients

A bowl of mutton biryani typically includes mutton (lamb or goat), basmati rice, onions, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and various spices like garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and cumin. Other common ingredients are ghee, mint leaves, coriander leaves, and sometimes potatoes, tomatoes, and fried onions.
